CONAN GREY

Conan Gray self-released his debut single "Idle Town", in March 2017. The song gained over 14 million streams
on Spotify and 12 million views on YouTube.[13][11] Gray self-released his second single "Grow" on September 1, 2017, with the music video
following the next day.[14][15] In October 2018, Gray released the single "Generation Why" on Republic Records,[13]
\ which was described as "a rallying call to millennials."[16] In November 2018, Gray released the five-track EP Sunset Season which includes
the songs "Idle Town", "Generation Why",
"Crush Culture", "Greek God", and "Lookalike". The EP peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard Heatseekers Albums chart and at No. 116 on the Billboard 200.[17][18]
Gray embarked on a North American tour in support of the EP with Girl in Red.[19] Shorty Award for Best YouTube Musician,[29] and was nominated for Breakthrough Artist at the 2019 Streamy Awards.[30]
